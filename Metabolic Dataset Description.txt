Metabolic Dataset Description

The dataset contains 4,340 entries and 21 columns, providing comprehensive data on individuals related to metabolic syndrome and associated health factors.

Column Descriptions:
codesub: A unique identifier for each individual (e.g., "R-S615112").
age: Age of the individual in years (integer).
hpt: Indicates the presence of hypertension ("yes" or "no").
smoking: Smoking status, such as "never smoked" or "still smoking".
dmdx: Indicates if the individual has diabetes ("yes" or "no").
height: Height of the individual in meters (float). Missing values exist.
weight: Weight of the individual in kilograms (float). Missing values exist.
waist: Waist circumference in centimeters (float). Missing values exist.
hip: Hip circumference in centimeters (float). Missing values exist.
msbpr: Mean systolic blood pressure in mmHg (float).
mdbpr: Mean diastolic blood pressure in mmHg (float).
hba1c: Glycated hemoglobin (HbA1c) percentage, indicative of long-term blood glucose levels (float). Missing values exist.
fbs: Fasting blood sugar levels in mmol/L (float). Missing values exist.
mogtt1h: 1-hour glucose tolerance test result in mmol/L (float). Missing values exist.
mogtt2h: 2-hour glucose tolerance test result in mmol/L (float). Missing values exist.
totchol: Total cholesterol level in mmol/L (float). Missing values exist.
ftrigliz: Fasting triglyceride levels in mmol/L (float). Missing values exist.
hdl: High-density lipoprotein (HDL) cholesterol in mmol/L (float). Missing values exist.
ldl: Low-density lipoprotein (LDL) cholesterol in mmol/L (float). Missing values exist.
gender: Gender of the individual ("male" or "female").
crural: Urban or rural residency status (e.g., "rural").
